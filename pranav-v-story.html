Pranav's Story

This is my story to share how I helped children in India take their first steps with new medical devices and proper medical treatment. I will be holding a series of fundraisers to meet my goal of $1,000 in order to sponsor 7 medical camps in rural areas in Gujarat, India. At these camps, children with disabilities will be diagnosed and referred for further treatment at the Polio Foundation, an LLF partner hospital.

What is a story?

Each of Lotus Life's volunteer has a story to tell! Essentially, a story starts with a volunteer's goal: it could be raising money for Lotus Life doing a marathon or throwing a birthday party. The volunteer then shares the story with us and raises the funds directed towards a certain project (it could be medical, education, or research and development). We then finish the story for them, telling them exactly where their funds went, whose lives they changed, and pictures of how it all happened!

What is the Lotus Life Foundation?

The Lotus Life Foundation aims to empower the lives of children with disabilities in rural India through medical intervention and education. Ultimately, their goal is to integrate these children into society and take a strong step towards eradicating the stigma associated with disability in rural India.
